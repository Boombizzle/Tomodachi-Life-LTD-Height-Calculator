What is this?

In Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream, it can be hard to line up your mii's height to real life measurements.

This calculator lets you know how many clicks of your d-pad you need to do from which point on the slider to calculate the real life height of any mii!

How do I use it?

Input your height in either Centimeters or Feet and Inches in the "Height:" input boxes.

The information box at the bottom will inform you of how many notches (or inputs) you are away from the desired height.

You can also use the actual slider if you prefer, and the input box will let you know what height that point on the slider is.

Why a 140cm (4' 7") to 203.5cm (6' 8") scale?

I chose these heights based on what seemed reasonable for the slider.

The slider has 127 notches, and I decided making each notch 0.5 cm was the best course of action. I found this scale managed to cover the widest spectrum of realistic heights, while also leaving a realistic disparity between heights.

I apologise if your character is smaller or taller than the heights covered.

Enjoy!
